Demoless: AI agent platform for creating live demos










Demoless sits on a company's website or in outbound campaigns and demos their product for them, 24/7. 


It learns from sales decks, product docs, past sales transcripts, live walkthroughs, internal wikis, and more. 


It runs real conversations with prospects, handles objections, qualifies leads, and books meetings. Sales teams wake up to warm pipeline with full conversation context instead of cold form fills. 


Every conversation makes Demoless  smarter. It learns new objection patterns, adapts to different buyer personas, and builds a deeper understanding of the product with each interaction.


Potential tech-stack:
* Simular’s computer use agent Sai
* Browserbase: Browsers, Search, Fetch, Stagehand, Browserbase CLI
* Redis: Caching, Redis Iris for agent memory, vector search, context retrieval
* Deepgram: STT, TTS, etc. 
* Sentry: Observability and/or error monitoring
